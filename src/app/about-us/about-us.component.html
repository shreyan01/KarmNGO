Our Mission

Karm NGO is dedicated to empowering communities and changing lives through sustainable development initiatives. We strive to create lasting positive impact in areas such as education, healthcare, and environmental conservation.

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Karm NGO began as a small group of passionate individuals determined to make a difference. Over the years, we've grown into a global organization, touching the lives of thousands across multiple countries.

Our journey has been one of continuous learning, adaptation, and unwavering commitment to our core values of compassion, integrity, and sustainability.
